Married to an Indonesian Citizen?
The Spouse Sponsored KITAS is the Visa you are looking for if you are a foreigner married to an Indonesian Citizen. There are two available options:

1. The Spouse Sponsored KITAS (Kartu Izin Tinggal Terbatus) which carries a one-year validity

2. The Spouse Sponsored KITAP (Kartu Izin Tinggal Tetap) which carries a 5-year validity if already 2 years married.
